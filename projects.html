Dive Aunties Foundation

A full redesign of diveaunties.org — migrating a Wix site to a custom Next.js platform for a 501(c) freediving nonprofit. The new site is still in active development; member flows, admin tools, and Stripe billing are shipping incrementally on the in-progress build.

  • Event management with RSVPs, capacity/waitlist, tier gating, and Google/Outlook/iCal export
  • Admin dashboard for members, events, and news — plus Stripe webhook sync for membership status
